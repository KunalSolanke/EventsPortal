Freestyle Online Group Dance Competition

“Dance is a way to find yourself and lose yourself at the same time.” A signature event of Alcheringa, Electric heels is a group dance competition, featuring the most talented crews across the nation. Your team, your grooves, your beats, our arena. Are you up for the challenge?"

    Rules
    • There is no registration fee for this event.
    • Teams consist of a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 25 members.
    • A minimum of 5 and a maximum of 16 members must be present in the frame at any given moment.
    • The minimum age limit for this competition is 12 years.
    • Any pre-recorded or live music can be used for the performance.
    • Creativity and Innovation will fetch extra points, Innovations such as props fetch extra points.
    • Performance duration should not exceed 8 minutes.
    • You have to submit the Google Drive Link of the video at www.alcheringa.in/registrations/ with provided access.
    • The decision taken by the organisers is final and binding for the performers.
    • Organizers have full authority to disqualify the participation in case of any discrepancy.
    • Vulgarity of any form will not be tolerated.So if a team feels that any act could be termed as vulgar, it is best suggested to discuss it to the event organiser.
